Common questions
Do I need to share my Google password? ▼
No. You add us as a Manager on your Google Business Profile — this never requires sharing your password. You keep full ownership and can remove us at any time from your Google account settings.
Will my customers know it's AI? ▼
No. Replies post directly from your Google account under your business name. There's no mention of ReviewForce or AI anywhere visible to customers.
Can I edit a reply after it posts? ▼
Yes. Since replies post from your own Google account, you can log into Google Business Profile and edit or delete any reply whenever you like.
How quickly will it go live? ▼
Once you've added us as a manager and submitted this form, we'll have everything connected within 24 hours. You'll get a confirmation email when it's live.
How do I cancel? ▼
Cancel anytime through your Whop account — it takes about 30 seconds. No lock-in contracts, no cancellation fees. Your subscription simply won't renew after the current billing period.
