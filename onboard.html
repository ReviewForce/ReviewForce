Common questions
Do I need to share my Google password? ▼
No. You add us as a Manager — you keep full ownership and can remove us at any time from your Google account. No passwords shared.
Will my customers know it's AI? ▼
No. Replies post from your own Google account under your business name. There's no mention of ReviewForce or AI visible to customers.
Can I edit a reply after it posts? ▼
Yes — since replies post from your Google account, you can log in and edit or delete any reply at any time.
How quickly will it go live? ▼
Once you've added us as manager and submitted this form, we'll have everything connected within 24 hours and send you a confirmation email.
