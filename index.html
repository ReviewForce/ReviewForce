FAQ

Common questions.

Is it safe to give ReviewForce access to my Google profile? +
Completely. You add ReviewForce as a manager on your Google Business Profile — you never share your password. You retain full ownership and can remove access instantly at any time from your Google account settings.
Will my customers know it's AI? +
No. Replies post from your Google account under your business name. The AI is calibrated to your brand voice so responses feel natural and personal. There is no mention of ReviewForce or AI visible to customers — ever.
Can I edit a reply after it's posted? +
Yes. Since replies post from your own Google account, you can log into Google Business Profile at any time and edit or delete any response. You always have full control.
What happens with a 1-star review? +
On Premium, you get an instant alert the moment a 1-star review comes in so you can personally step in. The AI still posts a professional, empathetic response automatically while you assess the situation.
How do I cancel? +
Cancel anytime through your Whop account — a few clicks, no forms, no calls. No contracts, no fees, no minimum commitment. Your subscription won't renew after the current billing cycle.
Is this compliant with Google's policies? +
Yes. ReviewForce replies on your behalf as an authorised manager of your Google Business Profile — the same way a staff member or marketing agency would. Fully within Google's permitted use policies.
