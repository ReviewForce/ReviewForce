About ReviewForce

ReviewForce is an AI-powered Google review management service operated in Victoria, Australia. We help small and medium-sized businesses automatically respond to Google reviews using artificial intelligence, posting replies directly to their Google Business Profile on their behalf.

By subscribing to ReviewForce, you agree to these Terms of Service in full. If you do not agree, please do not subscribe.

What We Provide

ReviewForce uses Magic Reply — a third-party AI platform — to generate review responses, which are then posted to your Google Business Profile through an authorised manager connection.

Replies are generated by AI and posted automatically without individual human review prior to publishing. You may edit or delete any reply at any time through your Google Business Profile account.

Service inclusions by plan:

  • Starter ($99/mo): AI replies within 24 hours, up to 50 reviews per month, monthly performance report, email support.
  • Premium ($119/mo): Unlimited AI replies within approximately 5 minutes, custom brand voice configuration, weekly performance report, 1-star review alerts, priority email support.
